Behind the Tech

Leveraging Bitcoin

At the core of Angor is Bitcoin, the original decentralized digital currency.

Nostr Integration

Angor uses Nostr for decentralized storage of project metadata and direct, uncensored communication between founders and investors.

Innovative Time-Lock Contracts

Time-lock contracts are central to Angor’s approach. They’re meant to improve accountability and trust by releasing funds at predetermined intervals, during which founders are expected to demonstrate tangible progress.

Taproot Advantages

By incorporating Bitcoin’s Taproot upgrade, Angor benefits from improved transaction privacy and efficiency.

Dual-Key Security

Investor and founder interests are aligned through a 2-of-2 multisig contracts.

Hash Locks for Token Issuance

Hash locks enable atomic token issuance, to transfer tokens within to investors. (this feature will be available in v2)

Stay in control of your investments with Angor

We’re shaking up how Bitcoin crowdfunding works. Angor is a decentralized platform, uniquely merging the security of Bitcoin with the transparency of Nostr. Investors funds are released in stages through time-lock contracts. Unspent funds can be recovered at any point, ensuring that investors maintain control and aligning the interests of investors and founders.
