Harnessing Bitcoin's Power

At the heart of Angor is Bitcoin, the original and most secure decentralized digital currency. Angor leverages Bitcoin’s robustness to ensure secure, transparent crowdfunding.

Seamless Nostr Integration

Angor integrates with Nostr, providing decentralized storage for project metadata and facilitating direct, uncensored communication between founders and investors.

Innovative Time-Lock Contracts

Time-lock contracts are central to Angor’s approach, releasing funds only as milestones are met. This system builds accountability and trust, ensuring that founders demonstrate progress at every stage.

Taproot-Enhanced Security

By utilizing Bitcoin’s Taproot upgrade, Angor offers enhanced transaction privacy and efficiency, providing users with a more secure and streamlined experience.

Dual-Key Multi-Signature Security

Investor and founder interests are safeguarded through 2-of-2 multi-signature contracts, aligning both parties’ incentives and ensuring mutual trust.

Future-Proof with Hash Locks

Hash locks enable atomic token issuance, allowing for secure token transfers to investors. This feature will be available in Angor v2, further enhancing the platform’s capabilities.
